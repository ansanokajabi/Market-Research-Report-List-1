<p><ul><li>Ordinary Reinforced Concrete Pole</li><li>Prestressed Concrete Pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete pylon market is segmented into two main types: ordinary reinforced concrete poles and prestressed concrete poles. Ordinary reinforced concrete poles are designed with traditional reinforcement methods, offering durability for utility applications. In contrast, prestressed concrete poles utilize high-strength steel tendons to enhance their load-bearing capacity and reduce structural deflection, making them suitable for tall structures and heavy loads. Both types are essential for supporting overhead power lines, telecommunications, and various infrastructure projects.</p></p>
