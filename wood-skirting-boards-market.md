<p><p>The wood skirting boards market is segmented into three main types based on density: Low Density Fiberboard (LDF), Medium-Density Fiberboard (MDF), and High Density Fiberboard (HDF). LDF is lightweight and easy to install, making it suitable for residential applications. MDF offers a balance of durability and affordability, ideal for a variety of interior designs. HDF, being the densest, provides superior strength and resistance, often used in commercial settings or high-traffic areas for lasting performance.</p></p>
