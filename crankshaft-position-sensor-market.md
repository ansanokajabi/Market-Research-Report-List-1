<p><ul><li>Linear Position Sensor</li><li>Rotary Position Sensor</li><li>Proximity Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crankshaft position sensor market includes various types: linear position sensors, rotary position sensors, and proximity sensors. Linear position sensors measure the distance of the crankshaft's linear movement, providing accurate data for engine timing. Rotary position sensors track the crankshaft's rotational position, essential for engine management systems. Proximity sensors detect the crankshaft's position without direct contact, enhancing reliability. Each type caters to different automotive applications, ensuring precise engine performance and optimizing fuel efficiency while minimizing emissions.</p></p>
