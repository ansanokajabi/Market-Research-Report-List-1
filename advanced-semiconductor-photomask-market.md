<p><p>The advanced semiconductor photomask market encompasses various types including quartz masks, soda lime masks, and relief plates. Quartz masks are utilized for their durability and minimal absorption of light, making them ideal for high-precision applications. Soda lime masks offer a cost-effective alternative with moderate performance, suitable for less demanding tasks. Relief plates, often used in experimental settings, create three-dimensional structures for specific lithographic processes. Each type serves distinct purposes based on performance requirements and manufacturing needs in semiconductor production.</p></p>
