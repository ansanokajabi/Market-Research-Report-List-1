<p><ul><li>Single Frequency</li><li>Dual Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fishing sensor market comprises two main types: single frequency and dual frequency sensors. Single frequency sensors operate at one specific frequency, making them suitable for basic depth and fish detection. In contrast, dual frequency sensors utilize two different frequencies, allowing for more detailed imaging and improved target resolution. This dual capability enhances the accuracy of fish identification and bottom structure analysis, catering to both recreational and commercial fishing needs. Overall, dual frequency sensors offer a more versatile and comprehensive solution.</p></p>
