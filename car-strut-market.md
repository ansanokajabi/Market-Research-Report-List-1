<p><strong>The Car Strut Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coilover Strut</li><li>Gas Charged Strut</li><li>Pneumatic Strut</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car strut market features several types designed for specific performance and comfort needs. Coilover struts combine a coil spring with a shock absorber, allowing for adjustable ride height and enhanced handling. Gas charged struts utilize pressurized gas to improve responsiveness and reduce foaming, providing better stability during driving. Pneumatic struts use air pressure to adjust ride quality and height, commonly found in luxury vehicles for a smooth driving experience. Each type caters to different preferences and vehicle requirements.</p></p>
