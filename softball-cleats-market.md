<p><p>The softball cleats market is divided into two primary types: soft ground and hard ground cleats. Soft ground cleats feature longer, detachable studs designed for softer, muddy fields, providing better traction and stability. In contrast, hard ground cleats have shorter, molded studs suitable for harder surfaces, offering necessary grip without causing excessive pressure on the feet. Players choose between these types based on field conditions to enhance performance, reduce injury risk, and ensure optimal comfort during gameplay.</p></p>
