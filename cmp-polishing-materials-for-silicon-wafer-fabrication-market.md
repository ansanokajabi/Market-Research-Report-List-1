<p><ul><li>CMP Slurry</li><li>CMP Pads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMP polishing materials for silicon wafer fabrication consist primarily of CMP slurry and CMP pads. CMP slurry is a liquid abrasive mixture used to remove surface materials on silicon wafers, enhancing smoothness and planarity. It typically contains abrasive particles, chemicals, and surfactants tailored for specific applications. CMP pads, on the other hand, provide the physical substrate for polishing, facilitating optimal contact and distribution of the slurry. Together, these materials play a crucial role in semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring the production of high-quality wafers essential for electronic devices.</p></p>
